Core Tenants

Scripture

Scripture

Scripture is G-d's word that brings light into our lives and guides us. G-d is a loving shepherd and we are His sheep. We follow His voice.

Psychology

Psychology

G-d encourages us to understand ourselves and the world He gave us. So we use science-backed methods in our counseling.

Journey

Journey

Faith is not in a vacuum. We integrate scriptures, psychology, and lived experiences to help form your healing journey.

Specialities

Trauma

Trauma

Trauma can be debilitating, making one feel trapped and responsible for their own pain. We can help you unpack the guilt and shame that often accompany trauma and help you learn exercises to heal your mind and body so you can bask in the light of G-d's love.

Marriage

Marriage

Marriage is hard. Sometimes a spouse commits adultery, sometimes a spouse is abusive, and sometimes a spouse is just drastically different than you. We can help you navigate the challenges of marriage and help you find the path to healing and restoration.

Anxiety and Depression

Anxiety and Depression

Anxiety and Depression can make you feel stuck while the whole world keeps flowing around you. There are many causes, and you don't have to walk through them alone. We can help you identify the root causes of your anxiety and depression, and provide you with the tools you need to overcome them.

Addiction

Addiction

Being unable to stop an addiction on your own is not your fault. Your brain has become hardwired to require certain substances or behaviors to feel normal. We can help you rewire your brain to live in Biblical truth and freedom.
